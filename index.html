Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Contaxon (we) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our application.

Information We Collect

  • Google/GitHub Profile Data: When you sign in using Google or GitHub, we collect basic profile information such as your name and email address.
  • Contact Information: If you create or manage contacts inside the app, their names and email addresses are stored securely to enable communication via email.
  • Login Password: If you register using email and password, your password is stored in encoded (hashed) format and never in plain text.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your login using OAuth2 (Google/GitHub)
  • To send emails using the Google SMTP service on your behalf (if applicable)
  • To send email notifications to the contacts you create within the application
  • To improve the app experience and provide support if needed

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs (for login and email sending)
  • GitHub OAuth (for login only)

These services may collect data as per their own Privacy Policies.

Data Security

All personal data is securely stored. Passwords are never stored in plain text and are encoded using strong hashing algorithms. Google SMTP credentials are securely managed for sending emails.
